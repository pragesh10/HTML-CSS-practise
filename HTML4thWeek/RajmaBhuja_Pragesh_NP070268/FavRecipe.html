Rajma Bhuja Recipe

Recipie Description

Rajma aka Kidney Beans are one of the most liked dish throughout the indian subcontinent. It is a comfort food for many just like it is to me. It is cooked in a thick, flavorful tomato-onion gravy and served with steamed rice(mostly). Rajma is cooked in a pressure cooker and then transferred to a pan where its gravy is prepared to give it a creamy, tasty flavour burst.

List of ingredients

  1. Rajma aka Kidney Beans
  2. Onion
  3. Garlic
  4. Chilli (Green)
  5. Ginger
  6. Jeera
  7. Oil (Mustard or even ghee)
  8. Garam Masala
  9. Turmeric
  10. Tomato Puree
  11. Salt
  12. Coriander

List of steps

First boil Rajma in a pressure cooker. Meanwhile, blend onion, garlic, ginger and green chilli in a blender. Take a pan, place it in thr stove, put some oil of your choice (or ghee) and saute some jeera and the paste you just made. After a while add some red chilli (optional), Coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala. After a while add some tomato puree and salt (according to your choice.)
Now finally add the boiled rajma along with some water and let it boil for a while. Meanwhile, Cook some basmati or any rice of your choice and cook it.
There you have it, Enjoy!
